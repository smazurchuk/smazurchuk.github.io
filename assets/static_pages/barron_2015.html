Barron (2015)

This paper claimed the results:
1. Discriminant Analysis using left thalamo-hippocampal and right thalamo-entorhinal cortex achieved 85% sensitivity and 91% specificity
2. Logistic regression (adding in two more connections) improved the results to 86% sensitivity and 100% specificity
This notebook checks if this result generalizes to our dataset. We cannot recreate "predictor 4" but we do have the other 3 predictors in our parcellation

Verify Parcel Names

for i=1:height(dataTr)

data = [double(Y), pred1', pred2'];

As we can see, result from the paper does not appear to hold in our dataset. Adding in the third predictor did not change the results.
